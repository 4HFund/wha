Guests & Visitors

Guest Responsibilities

  • You are responsible for the behavior of your guests at all times.
  • Do not let anyone into the building that you do not personally know or host.
  • Overnight guest limits and reporting requirements apply per WHA policy.
  • Allowing banned visitors onto the property can result in lease action.
Housekeeping

Unit Standards

  • Units must be reasonably clean, sanitary, and free of hazards.
  • Trash must be bagged and disposed of in designated areas.
  • Excess clutter, blocked exits, or pest infestation can result in violations and re-inspections.
Criminal Activity

Zero Tolerance

  • Drug-related or violent criminal activity can lead to lease termination.
  • Illegal activity anywhere on the property is taken seriously.
  • Please report suspected criminal activity to 911 and the office.
Rent & Charges

Rent & Air Conditioning

  • Rent is due on the 1st of each month (late if not received by policy deadline).
  • Air conditioner charges apply per month while installed.
  • Resident-caused damage may result in repair charges.
Inspections

Inspections & Entry

  • Annual and special inspections are required by HUD and WHA.
  • Reasonable notice will be given when possible, except in emergencies.
  • Refusing entry may result in lease enforcement steps.
Grievances

Grievance Rights

  • You may request an informal hearing or grievance if you disagree with certain decisions.
  • Requests must be made in writing within the time limits in the policy.
Request a Hearing

üêæPet Policy (Summary)

This is a summary only. The official WHA Pet Policy and your lease control any questions.

Allowed Pets

Type & Size

  • Common household pets such as small dogs, cats, birds, and fish.
  • Weight limit: generally up to 25 lbs for dogs and cats.
  • Pets must be registered with management before being brought into the unit.

Service animals and assistance animals are handled as reasonable accommodations and are not subject to pet fees, but must still follow behavior rules.

Not Allowed

Prohibited Types

  • Reptiles, rodents (other than approved small caged animals if allowed), insects, and wild animals.
  • Certain breeds such as pit bulls, rottweilers, chows, and boxers are not permitted.
  • Any animal that is aggressive, unsafe, or disruptive may not remain on the property.
Requirements

Registration & Care

  • Pets must be spayed/neutered by required age (unless medically excused).
  • Current vaccination and local license records must be provided.
  • Owners are responsible for cleaning up after pets and preventing damage.
Request Pet Registration Packet

üö´Ban Policy (Summary)

Certain individuals may be banned from all WHA property to protect residents, staff, and visitors.

Who Can Be Banned

Reasons for Bans

  • Violent or threatening actions toward residents, staff, or guests.
  • Drug-related or other criminal activity on or affecting WHA property.
  • Vandalism, repeated disturbances, or creating a public nuisance.
  • Loitering or being on property with no legal right to be there.
Rules

Reporting & Enforcement

  • Report banned individuals to the office or security immediately.
  • Residents are not allowed to invite or host banned individuals.
  • Violations can lead to lease action.

Resident of the Month Celebrate great neighbors

Nominate a Neighbor

Recognize residents who brighten the building, help others, or embody community values. Share a quick story so we can celebrate them.

Need help?

Questions? Call or text (304) 215-2584 or email sidney@wheelingwv-pha.org.

Nominations are reviewed monthly by management.

What to include

  • Name and apartment of the person you’re nominating.
  • A quick example of how they help the community.
  • Your contact info if we can follow up.

Great reasons to nominate

  • Helping neighbors with errands or rides.
  • Keeping shared spaces tidy or welcoming newcomers.
  • Positive attitude that brightens the building.
Thank you for celebrating your neighbors!

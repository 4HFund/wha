What to include

  • Location of the issue (building, floor, or unit if known).
  • What is happening and how often it occurs.
  • Your contact info if you want follow-up. Leave blank to stay anonymous.
Step 1

Share the concern

Describe what you saw or heard. Include dates, times, and locations if possible.

Step 2

Decide if you want contact

Add your name and phone number for updates. You can stay anonymous and we will still review.

We review promptly

We route safety concerns right away. Non-urgent items are reviewed within one business day.

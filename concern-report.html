Safety & neighbor issues Anonymous option

Report a Concern

Tell us about noise, nuisance, or safety concerns. You can stay anonymous. The information goes directly to Luau Manor staff.

Start the concern form Call/Text the office If life threatening, call 911 first

For urgent building safety after hours, call Emergency Maintenance at (304) 242-2820.

What to include

  • Location of the issue (building, floor, or unit if known).
  • What is happening and how often it occurs.
  • Your contact info if you want follow-up. Leave blank to stay anonymous.
Step 1

Share the concern

Describe what you saw or heard. Include dates, times, and locations if possible.

Step 2

Decide if you want contact

Add your name and phone number for updates. You can stay anonymous and we will still review.

Step 3

We review promptly

We route safety concerns right away. Non-urgent items are reviewed within one business day.

Tell us about your concern

Your message goes directly to the office. Anonymous submissions are welcome, and we send a confirmation if you include your email.

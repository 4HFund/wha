Pet Policy (Summary)

Rules for pets and assistance animals

This summary is for quick reference. The official WHA Pet Policy and your lease control any questions.

Open pet policy folder Jump to registration forms

Allowed pets

  • Common household pets such as small dogs, cats, birds, and fish.
  • Weight limit generally up to 25 lbs for dogs and cats.
  • Pets must be registered with management before being brought into the unit.

Service animals and assistance animals are handled as reasonable accommodations and are not subject to pet fees, but must still follow behavior rules.

Prohibited types

  • Reptiles, rodents (other than approved small caged animals if allowed), insects, and wild animals.
  • Certain breeds such as pit bulls, rottweilers, chows, and boxers are not permitted.
  • Any animal that is aggressive, unsafe, or disruptive may not remain on the property.

Registration & care

  • Pets must be spayed/neutered by required age (unless medically excused).
  • Current vaccination and local license records must be provided.
  • Owners are responsible for cleaning up after pets and preventing damage.

Pet forms, policy, and how to submit

Everything lives in one place so you do not have to hunt around. Open the pet folder to download the official policy, registration packet, and assistance animal forms.

  • Pet policy PDF and addenda are stored with the forms so you can review requirements and fees.
  • Registration packet includes the application, vet form, and caretaker form. Email the completed packet to sidney@wheelingwv-pha.org.
  • Assistance animal requests are handled with the reasonable accommodation paperwork in the same folder.

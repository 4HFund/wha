Maintenance Request

Use this form for non-emergency repairs. We review requests the same business day and schedule by urgency.

Start the form Call/Text (304) 215-2584 Emergency maintenance

Emergencies include no heat in cold weather, flooding, electrical sparks, or broken exterior doors/windows. Call (304) 242-2820.

What to expect

  • Tell us the room, issue, and best way to reach you.
  • We triage urgent repairs within 24 hours when possible.
  • Routine repairs are scheduled for the next available window.
Step 1

Share details

Include your name, unit, and the best phone number. You can also upload or text photos of the issue.

Step 2

Pick urgency

Select “within 24 hours” for urgent but non-emergency items like heavy leaks or loss of power to a room.

Step 3

Confirm access

Permission to enter lets maintenance work even if you are out. We will still knock first.

Submit your maintenance request

Your request goes directly to Luau Manor maintenance. Expect a confirmation and follow-up within one business day.

For emergencies, please call (304) 242-2820 instead of using this form.

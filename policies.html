Community Rules

Highlights from the Luau Manor rules, housekeeping standards, and reminders for shared spaces.

Read the handbook summary Ask for printed rules

Respectful community basics

  • Quiet hours keep hallways calm—please be mindful of neighbors.
  • Keep exits, hallways, and stairwells clear for safety.
  • Only approved pets are allowed. Register animals with the office.
  • Call maintenance when something breaks—early reporting helps.

Everyday reminders

  • No smoking inside units or shared areas.
  • Trash should be tied and placed in designated bins.
  • Guests must follow community rules; overnight guests may require notice.
  • Report disturbances or safety issues via the concern form.

Need clarification?

Call the office or request the full policy packet. We are happy to explain any rule.
